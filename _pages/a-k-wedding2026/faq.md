<section class="wedding-card">
  <h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>
  <p class="rsvp-instructions">We are so excited to celebrate with you. Here are a few quick answers to common questions.</p>

  <details>
    <summary>What is the attire?</summary>
    <p>We are aiming for business casual. For the gentlemen, no suits necessary (Álvaro will be wearing khakis). For the ladies, just try to avoid sparkly gowns. Please wear whatever helps you feel comfortable, confident, and ready to celebrate.</p>
  </details>

  <details>
    <summary>What food will be served?</summary>
    <p>Dinner, dessert, and drinks will be served. If you have dietary needs or allergies, please include them when you RSVP so we can plan accordingly.</p>
  </details>

  <details>
    <summary>Can I bring a date?</summary>
    <p>Due to limited space, we are only able to accommodate guests formally listed on each invitation. If you received a plus one, it will appear with your invitation details when you RSVP.</p>
  </details>

  <details>
    <summary>Can we bring our children?</summary>
    <p>Invitations include the names of everyone invited. If children are included, they will be listed with your invitation when you RSVP.</p>
  </details>

  <details>
    <summary>What happens if I do not RSVP in time?</summary>
    <p>If we do not receive your RSVP by the deadline, we will need to mark your response as not attending so we can provide final counts to our vendors.</p>
  </details>

  <details>
    <summary>Will there be an open bar?</summary>
    <p>Yes. We will have an open bar during the celebration. Please plan ahead for a safe ride home.</p>
  </details>

  <details>
    <summary>What time should I arrive at the ceremony?</summary>
    <p>Please arrive by 4:30 PM so you have time to park, settle in, and be seated before the ceremony begins.</p>
  </details>

  <details>
    <summary>Is there parking onsite?</summary>
    <p>There is limited onsite parking available for guests. We encourage to ride-sharing where possible to minimize our carbon footprint.</p>
  </details>

  <details>
    <summary>Where can I find the full timeline?</summary>
    <p>You can find the full event timing in the <a href="{{ '/a-k-wedding2026/schedule/' | relative_url }}">Schedule</a> tab.</p>
  </details>

  <details>
    <summary>Where is the wedding taking place?</summary>
    <p>The ceremony and celebration are in Pittsburgh, PA. See the <a href="{{ '/a-k-wedding2026/location/' | relative_url }}">Location</a> tab for details.</p>
  </details>

  <details>
    <summary>How should I RSVP?</summary>
    <p>Please use the <a href="{{ '/a-k-wedding2026/rsvp/' | relative_url }}">RSVP</a> tab and submit using the phone number associated with your invitation.</p>
  </details>

  <details>
    <summary>Can I update my RSVP after I submit?</summary>
    <p>Yes. You can resubmit your RSVP with the same phone number, and your latest response will overwrite the prior one.</p>
  </details>
